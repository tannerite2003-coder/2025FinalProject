Superman

Man of Steel

Clark Kent is the mild-mannered reporter — but when Metropolis needs hope, he becomes Superman.
Faster than a speeding bullet, more powerful than a locomotive.

Origin

Born Kal-El on the planet Krypton, he was sent to Earth as an infant. Raised by the Kents in Smallville, he learned to use his incredible powers for good.

Abilities
  • Super strength
  • Flight
  • Heat vision
    • X-Ray vision
    • Laser vision!!!
  • Invulnerability
  • Super hearing
Fun fact

Despite his strength, his secret identity relies on simple choices: glasses and an unassuming smile.
